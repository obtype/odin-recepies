Chicken Chilli

This is chicken chilli. I love chicken chilli.

The reason i love chicken chilli is because I love chicken and I also happen to love spice

Ingredients

  • 5 Green chilli
  • 500 grams of Chicken
  • A lot of Water

Recipe

  1. Boil the Water.
  2. Add the chicken in once the water starts to boil.
  3. Boil chicken for 10 minutes.
  4. Add spice.
  5. Lower the heat for the next 10 minutes.
  6. Pour out into your plate and eat!
