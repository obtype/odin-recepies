Lasagna

This is Lasagna. I love Lasagna.

The reason I love Lasagna is because I love pasta and I also happen to love meat, in layers

Ingredients

  • 5 pasta
  • 500 grams of ground meat
  • A lot of white sauce

Recipe

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Layer it in a dish.
  3. Cook the ground meat.
  4. Add it to the dish (in between the layers of pasta).
  5. Pre-heat the oven for 5 minutes at 80 degrees Celcius.
  6. Add dish to oven and cook for 10 minutes
  7. Take out the dish and eat!
